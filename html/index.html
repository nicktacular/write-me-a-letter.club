Write Me A Letter

Wait, what?

That's right, folks. I want you to write me a letter and I want to you do it in style. I don’t want you to write me a Slack message, DM, e-mail, text message, or anything else that causes electrical signals to propagate through some copper wire somewhere. Rather, I want you to stretch out some of your finger muscles and reach for a dusty pen, go get a clean, crisp piece of paper, sit down, collect your thoughts, and start writing. Don’t stop writing, don’t scribble anything out, and just let it all out. When you’ve finished, get an envelope, address it to me, get a stamp, and send the whole kit and kaboodle off to your local post office.

I promise that I will read it and very likely respond.

That’s it. No call-to-action, no buttons, no links. Just start writing…

Thank you for your attention. But why are you still here. Go and start writing!
